Welcome to Zachary's Gallery Shoot 'Em Up!

You control a white reticle on the sreen with your mouse.

Press the left mouse buttom to shoot at the screen.  Shooting the targets will add up on the top right on the screen.

Destroy at least 20 targets to win the game!  But be careful, you have 15 seconds to do this.  Have fun!
